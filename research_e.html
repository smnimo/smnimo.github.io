Research Interests

Human Computer Interaction

My focus is Human-Computer Interaction. Particularly I am interested in the system that can proactively assist users' decision making based not only on users' explicit input but also on users' non-verbal behavior (e.g., eye movements). Especially, I focus on how the system estimates users' internal states from gaze and changes action to users based on estimated internal states.
